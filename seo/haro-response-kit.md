## Notes

- **Speed beats perfection.** A 120-word response sent in 45 minutes beats a 400-word essay sent in 4 hours. Journalists on HARO are often on deadline.
- **Data beats opinion.** Every response should include at least one number from Gistify's actual product. "We saw X on our platform" is infinitely more credible than "In my experience..."
- **Offer an asset.** Always end with something the journalist can use: a dataset, a one-off screen, a demo, or a follow-up quote. It extends the conversation.
- **Track everything.** Maintain a spreadsheet of journalist names, query topics, response dates, and outcomes. A "no" today is a warm lead tomorrow.
