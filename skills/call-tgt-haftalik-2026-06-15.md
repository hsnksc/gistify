- **Mitigation:** CALL expiration July = time for macro volatility to settle.

### 2. Retail Sales Miss = Narrative Break
- TGT'nin recent rally'si "consumer demand recovering" üzerine. Retail Sales June 17'de negatif veya flat gelirse, bu narrative çöker. Stock -3%–-5% aynı gün.
- **Mitigation:** Stop loss at 50% premium. Position size small (%1–%2).

### 3. FOMC Hawkish Dot Plot
- Warsh'ın ilk FOMC'si. Reputation risk = hawkish tilt. Dot plot median 0 cut'a kayarsa, 10-year yield yükselir, retail multiples compress.
- **Mitigation:** Spread kullanarak max loss'ı define et.

### 4. Sector Rotation: Discretionary Outflow
- SSGA Q2 2026 Sector Perspectives: **Consumer Discretionary downgraded to Negative**. Low/middle-income household strain + savings rate düşük. TGT, WMT'den daha discretionary-heavy.
- **Mitigation:** TGT = long-term turnaround story; short-term sector headwind = timing risk.

### 5. Q2 2026 Comparison Cliff
- CFO, Q1 earnings call'da uyardı: "Q2 faces the hardest comparison of the year, nearly 2 percentage point difference as we lap Nintendo Switch 2 launch." Q2 2026 (Ağustos rapor) için comp sales riski yüksek. Bu, Ağustos expiration CALL'ları için önemli. July expiration = before Q2 earnings, safer.

### 6. Tariff & Margin Pressure
- TGT, WMT'den daha fazla discretionary mix + lower supplier leverage. Tariff pass-through = price hike = unit demand destruction. Gross margin 2025'te 30 bps decline yaşadı. Q1 2026 +80 bps rebound = fragile.
- **Risk:** Trade policy flare-up (China/Iran) = margin pressure return.

### 7. Activist & Governance
- Insider CEO (Fiddelke) = some activists unsatisfied. Board seat battles = distraction. Eğer Q2 2026 weak gelirse, activist pressure artar.

### 8. Juneteenth Liquidity
- 18 Haziran Perşembe = holiday öncesi thin volume. Options spread'leri genişleyebilir, slippage artabilir. Market-makers risk-off = closing positions harder.
- **Mitigation:** July expiration = no near-term liquidity crunch. Avoid 18 June weekly expiry.
