> Complete, ready-to-send email templates for influencer partnership outreach. Three tiers + follow-ups for each.

## TIER 1: Micro-Influencers (5K–50K followers)

**Target:** 30-40 influencers
**Value:** Free lifetime Pro + 30% affiliate commission
**Tone:** Personal, trader-to-trader, informal

---

**Subject:** Love your [specific content] — want to give you free access to something I built

**Body:**

Hey [Name],

Been following your [Twitter/YouTube/Discord] for a while. Your [specific post/video about topic] really stuck with me — especially the part about [specific detail]. Way too many traders talk theory; you actually show the setups and the P&L. Respect.

Gistify does the grunt work: real-time momentum scanning (50+ stocks, ranked by confluence), IV Rank tracking for earnings plays, CPR levels on every chart, and a clean UI that doesn't blind you at 4 AM.

**We'd love to give you free lifetime Pro access** — no strings, no payment info, no trial that expires. Plus a **30% affiliate commission** on anyone who signs up through your link. We don't do this for everyone; we want partners who actually trade and actually teach.

If you're open to it, I can set up your account in 2 minutes. No need to post about it until you've used it and decided it's worth sharing. We're not looking for a scripted promo — just honest feedback from someone who trades daily.

What do you say?

[Your Email] | [Your Twitter/X handle]

**Target:** 15-20 influencers
**Value:** Free lifetime Pro + affiliate + exclusive preview + co-branded content
**Tone:** Formal but still personal, respectful of their time

---

**Subject:** Partnership opportunity: Gistify x [Name] — co-branded content + revenue share

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

My name is [Your Name], and I'm reaching out on behalf of Gistify — a trading workspace built for active options traders. I've been following your content for [timeframe], and your approach to [specific strategy/topic] is exactly the kind of honest, data-driven analysis our users value.

Our momentum scanner filters 50+ stocks in real-time using VWAP, CPR, IV Rank, and confluence scoring. Our earnings module tracks IV crush plays, expected moves, and historical realized vol. We built it because we couldn't find a tool that combined speed with depth.

**What we're offering:**

1. **Free lifetime Pro access** — full feature set, no limits
2. **30% affiliate commission** on all paid referrals through your unique link
3. **Exclusive preview access** to new features before public release (e.g., our upcoming options flow module)
4. **Co-branded content** — we'd love to produce a "[Name] x Gistify" strategy guide or video series that you own and distribute as you wish

**What we're looking for:**
- Authentic use and honest feedback (no scripted content)
- Occasional mention if you find it genuinely useful
- Nothing forced — if it doesn't fit your content, no problem

I'd love to get on a quick call to walk you through the platform and see if there's a fit. Here's my calendar: [Calendly/link]

If you're interested but short on time, I can also send a 5-minute Loom demo instead.

Looking forward to hearing from you.
