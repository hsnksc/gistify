### Full Description

```
Gistify is a dark-themed trading workspace built specifically for active options traders who are tired of jumping between 5 tabs every morning.

**What it does:**
- **Momentum Scanner:** Filters 50+ stocks in real-time using VWAP, CPR, RSI, MACD, and volume confluence. Ranked by setup quality. Updated every 15 minutes.
- **Earnings Module:** Tracks IV Rank, expected moves, and historical realized volatility for every earnings play. Pre-built setups: straddles, iron condors, spreads.
- **CPR Levels:** Central Pivot Range (S1, R1, central pivot) on every stock page. No manual calculations. No Excel.
- **Dark UI:** Easy on the eyes at 4 AM. High contrast for chart levels. Mobile-friendly for pre-market checks.

**Who it's for:**
Active options traders — day traders, swing traders, and earnings players who need speed, accuracy, and a clean workflow.

**Why we built it:**
We were tired of slow scanners, cluttered dashboards, and light-mode interfaces that burned our retinas. So we built the tool we actually wanted to use every day.

**Pricing:**
Free tier: Daily top 10 momentum plays, basic earnings calendar
Pro tier: Full scanner, unlimited watchlists, earnings playbook, advanced CPR ($29/month or $249/year)

Built by traders. No fluff. Just the data you need to make better trades.
```

## 8. 20 Anticipated Questions + Answers

### Product & Features

**Q1: Is Gistify a broker? Can I place trades through it?**
A: No — Gistify is a research and analysis workspace, not a broker. You analyze setups here, then execute through your broker. We integrate with most major brokers for data, but execution stays where it belongs.

**Q2: What data sources does Gistify use?**
A: Real-time market data from [provider], options data from [provider], earnings calendar from [provider], and our proprietary confluence scoring engine.

**Q3: How often does the momentum scanner refresh?**
A: Every 15 minutes during market hours (9:30 AM–4:00 PM ET). Pre-market and after-hours scans refresh every 30 minutes.

**Q4: What indicators are included in the confluence score?**
A: VWAP with standard deviation bands, CPR (S1/R1/central pivot), RSI, MACD, Bollinger %B, relative volume, and options flow skew. Each indicator is weighted based on historical backtesting.

**Q5: Can I create custom watchlists?**
A: Yes — Pro users get unlimited custom watchlists. Free users get one default watchlist with the daily top 10.

**Q6: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet — we're mobile-web responsive, which works great on phones and tablets. A native app is on the roadmap for Q4 2025.

**Q7: What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
A: Free gets you the daily top 10 momentum plays and basic earnings calendar. Pro gets the full 50+ stock scanner, unlimited watchlists, earnings playbook with IV Rank data, advanced CPR levels, and priority support.

**Q8: Can I export data from Gistify?**
A: Yes — Pro users can export watchlists, earnings data, and scan results to CSV. Excel-friendly formatting.

**Q9: Do you offer a trial for Pro?**
A: We don't do trials — we do free tier. Use the free tier as long as you want. If you find value, upgrade to Pro. If you don't, no hard feelings.

**Q10: What markets does Gistify cover?**
A: US equities and options only. NYSE, NASDAQ, and major ETFs (SPY, QQQ, IWM, etc.). No forex, crypto, or international markets yet.

### Pricing & Business

**Q11: How much is Pro?**
A: $29/month or $249/year (save $99). Free tier is free forever.

**Q12: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes — 14-day no-questions-asked refund on Pro subscriptions. If it's not for you, you get your money back.

**Q13: Is there a team or enterprise plan?**
A: Not yet — we're focused on individual traders right now. If you're a prop firm or trading group and need bulk access, email us at [email].

**Q14: How do you make money?**
A: Pro subscriptions. No ads, no selling data, no affiliate kickbacks from brokers. We build a tool worth paying for.

### Technical & Trust

**Q15: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes — we use industry-standard encryption, never store broker credentials, and don't sell or share user data. We're working toward SOC 2 compliance.

**Q16: Who built Gistify?**
A: Active options traders who were tired of slow, ugly tools. [Name] and [Name] are the co-founders — both trade daily and use Gistify as their primary workspace.

**Q17: How accurate is the confluence scoring?**
A: The scoring engine is backtested on 2 years of market data. No system is perfect — trading involves risk — but the score helps you prioritize, not predict.

**Q18: What brokers do you support for data?**
A: We pull market data directly from our providers, so you don't need a specific broker. For portfolio tracking, we support [list major brokers].

**Q19: Can I suggest features?**
A: Absolutely — we have a public roadmap and feature voting board. Our best features came from user suggestions. Drop your idea in the Discord or email us.

**Q20: What makes Gistify different from TradingView / Finviz / ThinkorSwim?**
A: TradingView is great for charts. Finviz is great for screening. ThinkorSwim is great for execution. Gistify combines scanning, earnings analysis, and setup tracking in one dark-themed workspace — built specifically for options traders who need speed. We're not trying to replace your broker or charting tool. We're the workspace that connects everything.
