## 📋 Executive Summary

AST SpaceMobile is a company providing direct space-based cellular broadband service to standard smartphones via its **BlueBird** satellites. Founded in 2017, headquartered in Midland, Texas. ### 1.2 Business Model and Technology

AST SpaceMobile operates with the vision of **the world's first and only space-based cellular broadband network**. BlueBird satellites carry massive phased array antennas that allow existing 4G/5G smartphones to connect **without modification**.

- **Block 1 BlueBird:** ~1,500 kg, ~64 m² antenna, ~21 Mbps test speed, 5,600 cells
- **Block 2 BlueBird:** ~6,500 kg, ~223 m² (2,400 sq ft) antenna, **120-200 Mbps** target, 2,000+ active cells
- **AST5000 ASIC:** Custom-designed satellite embedded chip, 10 GHz processing bandwidth
- **3,800+ patent / patent-pending**
- **MNO Revenue Share Model:** 50/50 revenue sharing with AT&T, Vodafone, Rakuten, etc.
