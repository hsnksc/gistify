10. Warsh's First FOMC — Unknown Known: 28-29 July, Warsh's first chairmanship meeting. No dot plot. Content unknown, date known. Historically, first meeting creates 1.5-2x normal volatility. VIX Jul 30 call spread = cheap hedge.
