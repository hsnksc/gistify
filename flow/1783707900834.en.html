GB300 NVL72 relies mainly on copper backplane and copper SerDes links for short-range intra-rack connections. Since physical signal losses aren't a problem at lower speeds, copper is the cost-effective, stable choice.

Modulator Technologies

MZM (Mach-Zehnder) — StandardThe industry standard with high thermal stability. But its large size and high insertion loss take up space in CPO integration.
MRM (Micro-Ring) — Nvidia CPO EngineExtremely compact and low-power. But so sensitive to temperature fluctuation that it depends on complex micro-heaters.
EAM (Germanium) — Marvell & Celestial AICombines MZM's thermal stability with MRM's compactness for the most optimal foundry-level packaging efficiency.

🏢 Marvell (NASDAQ: MRVL) — Platform Player

▾

Marvell is one of the two most critical intermediaries that let cloud providers design their own AI processors (Google TPU, AWS Trainium, Microsoft Maia) at the foundry level. It is advancing toward a unique platform-player position, integrating into both the ultra-short-range copper switching market and the next-generation optical network layer. Sector position: Custom ASIC and Network Solutions.
