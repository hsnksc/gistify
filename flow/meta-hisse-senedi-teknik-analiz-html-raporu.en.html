⚠️ Meta Compute SucceedsHigh Reward · 40%

Conditions: 10,000+ customers by end of 2026, $5B+ annual revenue, 40%+ gross margin

Impact: $800–$900 price target, 12-18 months, 35x–40x P/E

Rationale: New revenue stream re-rating, ability to compete with AWS/Azure/Google Cloud

⚠️ Capex Spending EasesMedium · 35%

Conditions: 2027 capex guidance falls to $80-100B range, free cash flow improves

Impact: $700–$750 price target, 6-12 months, 30x–35x P/E

Rationale: Rising investor confidence, easing profitability concerns

⚠️ Muse Spark UnderdeliversHigh Risk · 20%

Conditions: Model performance below expectations, weak user adoption

Impact: $450–$500 price target, 3-6 months, 20x–25x P/E

Rationale: Erosion of AI leadership position, loss of investor confidence

⚠️ Regulatory Pressure RisesMedium · 25%

Conditions: New EU AI regulation, US antitrust litigation

Impact: $480–$550 price target, 3-9 months, 22x–28x P/E

Rationale: Rising operating costs, slower growth

⚠️ Macro RecessionHigh Risk · 15%

Impact: $400–$450 price target, 6-12 months, 18x–22x P/E

Rationale: Falling ad revenue, weaker consumer spending
