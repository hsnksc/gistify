🗣️ Dominant Narratives

▾
1. Structural Concentration: KOSPI is built on a single sector, two stocks. One sector (AI semiconductors), two stocks (Samsung + SK Hynix). This structural lack of diversification is the biggest risk.
2. Leverage Risk: Retail investors are trying to buy the dip with leverage. Margin balance 38 trillion won, forced selling 300 billion won. Leveraged ETFs grew 4x in one month.
3. AI Valuations: AI demand is real but valuations are "pricing for perfection". Broadcom guidance miss showed these expectations are fragile.
4. Broadcom Catalyst: Broadcom guidance miss = first crack in AI supercycle expectations. This triggered a recalibration in the global AI semiconductor trade.
5. Inverse Positioning: Foreigners are selling, retail is buying. Classic smart money vs dumb money divergence. Foreigners sold a record 44.7 trillion won in May.
6. Real Estate Link: Korean real estate market is under stock leverage spillover effect. Stock/bond-collateralized housing purchases rose from 3.7% to 6.6%.
7. Prestigious Fund Warnings: Wealspring/Banxia: the bubble argument is no longer mainstream only, prestigious funds are joining. Investors who foresaw the 2007 peak warned.
8. Regulation Behind: FSS regrets ETF launch, BOK "monitoring". Regulation cannot keep up with market speed. Weekly options launch was postponed.
