RSI 70+ overbought, potential fade after 6/29 gap-up, insider selling ($114M), and short interest increase (9.80%) are short-term weakness signals.
Options Perspective: For options traders, volatility plays are attractive with IV Rank 100% and Expected Move ±$45.52 (6.55%). However, each strategy involves directional risk and IV crush risk; position size should not exceed 5% of portfolio, minimum 2x return potential should be sought, and stop-loss must absolutely be placed.

Net outlook: AMAT is a strong company in a strong trend; but the price is 'pricing in a lot'. For current holders, a 'hold and watch' strategy; for new entries, wait for correction levels (S1 $690, S2 $680, 50D MA $524.58). August 13 earnings is a critical turning point that will shift the balance between scenarios.
