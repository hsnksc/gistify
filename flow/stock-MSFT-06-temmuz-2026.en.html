🤖 OpenAI + Anthropic: $100B Claim Debunked

▾

The $100B ARR claimed in the article is FALSE. Actual ~$72B ARR, but GAAP revenue is much lower.

Why the $100B claim is wrong: (1) OpenAI $25B + Anthropic $47B = $72B, not $100B. (2) The "ARR" label blends CARR, recognized ARR, and run-rate — the gap can reach 70% (Bessemer/TechCrunch). (3) GAAP revenue is only ~$17.5B. (4) Anthropic uses gross-basis accounting; IPO restatement could bring a 20–40% drop (FutureSearch). (5) There is double/triple counting in the ecosystem.
Microsoft Circular Revenue: Microsoft invested $13B+ in OpenAI, OpenAI spent $17.2B on Azure in 2025. Amazon invested $13B in Anthropic, received $100B AWS commitment. "AI revenue" is partly a return of investor capital.

💡 10 Cross-Dimension Insights

▾
1. Circular Revenue Trap: $13B investment in OpenAI → $17.2B returns to Azure. Anthropic $13B → $100B AWS. "AI revenue" is partly a return of investor capital. High GAAP restatement risk in S-1 filings.
2. Traffic-Revenue Paradox: Azure has 1.61% internet traffic but 21% revenue. 13x revenue-per-traffic. If Google TPU economics improve, price pressure may increase.
3. Excess Inertia Double-Edge: Protects Office but also prevents Microsoft from creating the new paradigm. Copilot = augmentation, not transformation.
4. Energy Bottleneck: Grid 18–36 month delay. $190B may not be fully deployable. This could unexpectedly improve FCF.
5. Regulatory Underpriced: EU DMA, UK CMA, US FTC. Not priced in valuation models. Fair value could be $30–50 lower.
6. Copilot Chasm: 20M seats but only 5% pilots scaling. $10.8B potential is stuck on integration, not price.
7. Platform Landlord: Microsoft does not own the AI model, it is an infrastructure tenant. Azure AI 40% margin but model owners take the premium. Lower aggregate margin.
8. OpenAI Exclusivity Loss: Ended in April 2026. 99% of $627B RPO is OpenAI effect. Ex-OpenAI RPO growth is only 26%. Azure AI moat is broken.
9. P/E Compression Paradox: 23x looks cheap. If growth falls to 15–18%, fair P/E is 17–20x. The discount is extremely sensitive to growth assumptions.
10. Consumer AI Blind Spot: Missed every consumer platform shift (mobile, social, short video). ChatGPT 900M weekly users. Microsoft is almost nonexistent in consumer AI.
